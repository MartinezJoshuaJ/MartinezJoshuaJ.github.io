Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

Who inspires you? An athlete you grew up watching? The author of your favorite book? Maybe it's a family member or your favorite high school teacher Personally, I have all the aforementioned inspirations in my life. I believe people should have different inspirations for instances of their lives.

My musical inspiration is Mozart. No, I do not play any instruments, but I love listening to music!

Mozart inspires me because:

  • He took a chance! He was the first composer to blend multiple styles together. Mozart is one of the reasons, that when you're scrolling on Spotify, you can pick from the 100s of genres.
  • He is well versed! Not only did he compose musical pieces, he also knew how to play 5 instruments!
    • That inspires me to add as many "tools" to my toolbag as I can as I progress throughout life.
